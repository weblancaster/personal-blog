I'm Michael Lancaster, a Staff & Lead Frontend Software Engineer building web and mobile applications at scale. Across my 13+ years of experience, I've architected micro frontend systems from scratch, managed high-performing engineering teams, and shipped mission-critical products for companies like Mission Lane, Endpoint, and BlackBerry. I even designed and launched a full-stack Go/React Native startup solo as a founder.

My focus is the intersection of clean architecture, product velocity, and developer experience. I specialize in React, Next.js, and TypeScript, building robust CI/CD pipelines and testing strategies to accelerate entire teams. Currently scaling external experiences at Mission Lane and building QuantaQuest AI for fun. Outside of code, I'm a Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu black belt—teaching me more about discipline and staying calm under pressure than any engineering book.
